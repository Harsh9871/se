Cyber Security Services

Established in 2017, Sohang specializes in offering top-notch cyber security solutions, providing technical and hardware software development support to government agencies such as Delhi Police 3rd BN., CRPF, and the Indian Army.

Comprehensive Security Solutions

Sohang Cyber Security Services extends its expertise in data security, server services, customized software development, backup solutions, and more. Our primary goal is to deliver the best security solutions to companies across various industries.

Specialized Software Design

In addition to security services, Sohang excels in software design, particularly catering to the needs of entities like Delhi Police. We also offer open-source services to enhance the efficiency and functionality of software applications.
